Perlen papier AG - interview

A little interview with an employee at Perlen Papier AG
  • How many apprentices does the Perlen Papier AG have? - 29
  • Where are our priorities? - Our main priorities are to reduce the green-house gas emissions to a maximum.
  • How would you describe our department? - We provide individual and reliable supervision. We are small but pretty efficient.
  • What are the sales per year? - About 529.7 Mio per year.
  • How many investors do we have? - About 13
  • How much paper do we produce per year? - We produce about 500k tons of paper per year.
