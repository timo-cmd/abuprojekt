Perlen papier AG - Timeline

  • The company was founded in 1873
  • Integration to the CPH Holding (1971)
  • Produced over 100'000 tons of paper (1971)
  • Perlen papier introduces the TMP (Thermo Mechanical Pulp) machine used for wood generation
  • Produces paper using the PM4 (Papier Maschine 4) (2000)
  • The new PM7 becomes one of the worlds most advanced paper machine. (2010)
  • Perlen papier becomes european leader in electricity and heat efficiency. (2015)


    • Perlen papier AG - Characteristics
